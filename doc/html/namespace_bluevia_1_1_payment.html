enum Bluevia::Payment::TransactionStatus

The status of the payment.

Enumerator:
SUCCESS 

The payment operatioin has been completed and the result is successful. The customer has been charged.

FAILURE 

The payment operation has been completed but the result is unsuccessful. The customer has not been charged.

PENDING 

The payment operation has not been completed yet. Ask again later.
